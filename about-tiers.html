Challenge List

Tiers

Beginner

As the name says it's where most people start doing challenges. It includes most vanilla Undertale and Deltarune fights such as Papyrus no hit, Spamton NEO, and Undyne. There are also some easy fangames in here as well like Trio's Squabble, Angelic Altercation, and Mike's Big Showdown. If you're looking to do full game challenges there's no heal runs of both Deltarune chapters individually.

Intermediate

Things here get harder, but still remain mostly casual-friendly. The tier contains no heal runs of more difficult fangames like Edgetale ZY, The Heaven, and SPMY Sans. There are also some well know fangames such as PS!Outertale, Undertale Yellow, and Disbelief Papyrus. As for full game runs, you have Deltarune Chapter 1 No Hit, and Deltarune Chapters 1+2 No Heal.

Advanced

The difficulty here gets more serious. The main run to represent it's difficulty is no hit Sans. Some fangames challenges are Dusttale: Last Genocide no hits, vengence route Ceroba no hit, and some Sad Bread games like Scampton the Great and Mike's Big Showdown. Advanced also has more full game runs then the previous two tiers. These include Deltarune Ch. 1 All Bosses no hit, Undertale Genocide and Neutral routes no heal, and a New Game+ Undertale no hit run.

Expert

Here, higher-level fangames are featured, most of which are no heal runs, but does include some easier fangames as no heal challenges. Most of these are no hits in higher tiers. Shanghaivania, Distrust 2, No More Deals, and Delirium of Aeons are all in this level as no heals. More casual fangames include Bad Time Trio, UTY Flowey Phase 1, and Disbelief Papyrus Phase 2 as no hits. Base game runs in this tier are Flowey final phase no hit and Deltarune Ch2 main route no hits.

Master

This tier is generally considered to be when the challenges become difficult even for very experienced players. This tier and tiers beyond are located in the Master's Sheet. Some no hit challenges recognizable to casual players include individual Zenith Martlet phases no hit, Photoshop Flowey No Hit, Deltarune Chapter 2 Snowgrave No Hit, Deltarune Chapter 2 All Recruits No Hit, and Deltarune Chapters 1+2 No Hit. A lot of ZYDCN games are also located in this tier like Edgetale and Underfell Plan.

Elite

This tier is a massive step up from the Master tier, being significantly more difficult and time consuming. Many challenges in this tier are a combination of many other smaller challenges that have been featured previously and because of that it's a big step up from master tier. Some examples of this are Deltarune Chapters 1+2 All Bosses No Hit, Martlet Full Fight No Hit, and Endingtale Full Fight No Hit. Other challenges from this tier include No More Deals No Hit, Keeptale No Hit, and Undertale Last Breath Phase 3 No Hit.
